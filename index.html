How to play colourdle

Colourdle is similar to games like wordle in the respect that you have to guess a sequence of colours. When you start playing, you can input your guess using the buttons at the bottom. when you guess, the colours will have a new border. Grey means that the colour is not in the colour sequence at all, anywhere, however amber means that colour is in the sequence, but not in that particular place. Green means it is in the sequence AND in the right place. For example:

In this guess little timmy has made here, red and purple are not in the sequence at all, however yellow is in the sequence and in the wrong place. Blue is in the sequence, and it is in the right place. If you guess the correct sequence, you win! On easy mode and medium mode, you have 5 guesses. On hard mode, you have 4 guesses.

Chips and wins

Chips are the currency of the game. You can earn chips by winning games. You can spend chips on items in the store (coming soon in next weeks update!). However, if you loose, you can actually loose chips. Lets look at how much you gain and loose for each mode:

Easy mode win: 5
Easy mode loss: -1
Medium mode win: 10
Medium mode loss: -2
Hard mode win: 20
Hard mode loss: -5
